## \[Papers\]

[Datacenter Ethernet and RDMA: Issues at Hyperscale](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2302.03337)

- Written by major players of UEC

- Introduces limitation of RoCEv2 and viable solution

[SIRD: A Sender-Informed, Receiver-Driven Datacenter Transport Protocol](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2312.15403)

- Going to be published in NSDI’25

- Receiver-driven congestion control algorithm. May similar to UEC congestion mgmt.

[REPS: Recycled Entropy Packet Spraying for Adaptive Load Balancing and Failure Mitigation](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2407.21625)

- Published by Microsoft

- Introducing “decentralized” adaptive routing implementation (only endpoints are involved).

  - Cache “good” paths within the RNIC, with minimal storage overhead.

  - Can react to the network failure as well

[STrack: A Reliable Multipath Transport for AI/ML Clusters](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2407.15266)

- Published by AMD/Microsoft

- A new transport protocol that is aware of packet-level multipathing

- Summary by Rachata: <a href="https://docs.google.com/document/d/1P31FZnPdWd9_5cPpPa_gz69a0NUHa34aJlF2Rl_TmNg/edit?tab=t.a0tfpn90n211" data-card-appearance="inline">https://docs.google.com/document/d/1P31FZnPdWd9_5cPpPa_gz69a0NUHa34aJlF2Rl_TmNg/edit?tab=t.a0tfpn90n211</a>
